How to Get Started

Follow these four simple steps to start bidding on unique items from around the world. It's fast, secure, and exciting!

1

Register Your Account

Click the "Login" button on our homepage to open the registration form. Fill in your details to create a free and secure account. This gives you full access to participate in any auction.

2

Discover Unique Items

Once registered, browse through hundreds of featured auctions on our main page. You can use the search bar to find specific items or filter by category to discover hidden gems and collectibles.

3

Place Your Bid

Found something you love? Click "View Details" to see more information. In the pop-up, enter a bid amount higher than the current bid and click "Place Bid." Watch the countdown and stay on top!

4

Win & Receive Your Item

If your bid is the highest when the auction timer ends, you're the winner! You'll be notified, and you can then arrange for payment and delivery. To check the winner, simply view the details of an ended auction.
